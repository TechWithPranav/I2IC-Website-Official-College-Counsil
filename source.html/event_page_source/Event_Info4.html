Agile Foundation Event

The Agile Event was conducted by I2IC Council and provided a diverse learning experience to all attendees. This session was conducted by Mr. Prashant Pund Sir who is the founder of AgileSoft Methodologies which is a company engaged in providing consultancy services in Agile approach and SDLC activities for Software Industry. There were no pre-requisites required for the course other than to have an open mind to learn about revolutionary approach.
The course covered four modules namely 'Methodology', 'Agile Project Management', 'Agile Flow Management' and 'Scaling Agile Management to Enterprise Level'. The event was conducted for the institutes of VIT, VIIT and VU. Overall the event offered a well-rounded and immersive learning experience, equipping attendees with a broad spectrum of skills and knowledge.
