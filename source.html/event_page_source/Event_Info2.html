LTIMindTree Industry Visit

A company visit for LTIMindtree was arranged on February 28, 2024, scheduled from 10:00 AM to 2:30 PM. Approximately 50 students from the first-year Computer and Software Engineering branch, along with 14 members from the I2IC council, participated in the visit. The guidance during the visit was provided by Mr. Vilas D Ghonge, Dr. Y.D. Deshpande, and Mrs. Swati Patil. The industry visit began with Anirban Mukherjee introducing the LTImindtree company, elucidating its creation through the amalgamation of LTI Infotech and Mindtree.
He also offered insights into the company's vision, client base, workforce, branches, and operational processes, along with providing guidance on placement preparation.Following this, Adarsh sir, an expert in cybersecurity, conducted an informative session on the subject. The session was enjoyable and highly engaging. We delved into numerous crucial aspects of the hiring process and received valuable guidance on preparing for future placements.

Mentor To Go Day 1(SY)

First day of the Mentor to go event organized by the I2IC council of VIIT. Mentor to Go is an NGO, where they help students get their mentors to help students in building their career. The session was based on providing career mentorship to young people anywhere, anytime across the length and breadth of India. Session was arranged in different time slots for different branches. The 1st day session is for SY Students of all branches from 10am to 4pm.
Guest speaker Mr. Pravin Jawale sir and Mr.Roshan Ghadi sir talked about how mentoring is important for people. They told stories about people who were successful because of their mentors. It is a personalized 6-month mentorship program tailored for students in any year apart from the final year. This program focuses on enhancing students' work-readiness skills. After that they explained the process for downloading and signing in Mentor to go app. 100+ students attained the session from each department.

Mentor To Go Day 2(TY)

Second day of the Mentor to go event is also organized by the I2IC council of VIIT. Session was arranged in different time slots for different branches. The 2nd day session is for TY Students of all branches from 9am to 2pm. Guest speaker Mr.Pravin Jawale sir talked about how mentoring is important for people.They told stories about people who were successful because of their mentors. His valuable speech motivated students to register for a mentoring online platform.
They performed various activities with students related to mentoring. It is a personalized 6-month mentorship program tailored for students in any year apart from the final year. Each student is matched with a personalized mentor based on factors such as domain, field of study, language, and gender. Further students gave a screening test in Mentor to go app. 100+ students attained the session from each department. Finally, the session ended with a vote of thanks by VP of I2IC Gaurav Lonari.
