Techsaksham Event

The event conducted by the I2IC Council was a comprehensive and diverse learning experience spanning over six days, from October 30th to November 4th. They dived into Web Technology, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript along with Relational Database Management.

Overall, the event offered a well-rounded and immersive learning experience, equipping attendees with a broad spectrum of skills and knowledge.

Resume Building Session

Resume Building session was organized by TPO through I2IC council of VIIT. This session was conducted by our guest speaker Mr. Neeraj Kumar Solanki. He is from career launcher team Pune, career launcher has got centers in 225 cities across 175 different locations across the country. Career Launcher has been training thousands of students with commendable results for the past 19 years. The session was for providing guidance on resume writing. The session was arranged from 10.15am to 12.15pm for all branches. The session was arranged for TY students.
Guest speaker Mr. Neeraj Kumar Solanki talked about how resume writing should be done and explained the importance of all contents of a professional resume. He explained what all things we should include in a resume and what we should avoid. He also showed several examples of resume for student reference. 150+ students attended the session.

Generative AI Workshop

The event conducted by the I2IC Council in collaboration with NXTWAVE provided guidance of generative AI model and various concepts related to AI. It covered the difference and applications of Artificial Narrow Intelligence and Artificial General Intelligence. The workshop covered a practical representation of developing a generative AI model. The key topic of this workshop was prompt Engineering. Honourable speaker also covered the core concepts such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Deep Learning along with their applications. It also covered topics based on Analytical AI and Generative AI. The main aim of the workshop was to develop Generative AI model and learn about prompt Engineering.
