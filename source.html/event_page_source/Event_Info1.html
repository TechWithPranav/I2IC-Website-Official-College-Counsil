BMC Orientation Session

BMC Software Company Orientation on Mainframe Technology was organized by TPO through I2IC council of VIIT. This session was conducted by our guest speaker Mr. Sandeep Sawant. He is from BMC Software Company Team. Mr. Sandeep Sawant gave many insights on Mainframe Technology. He also elaborated the placement process in this field. The session was for providing guidance on Mainframe Technology. Guest speaker Mr. Sandeep Sawant talked about how Mainframe Technology has been evolved through these many years.
Through this session students got to know about future scope in this field of technology and how mainframes s integrate with contemporary technologies such as cloud computing, big data, and analytics. Mr. Sandeep Sawant sir and his collegues also had a question and answers interaction part at last with all the students and helped them in getting to know more about this technology. 300+ students attained the session.

TCS Guest Speaker Session

The Institute Industry Interaction Cell (I2IC) successfully conducted the TCS guest session from 1 p.m. to 3 p.m. on August 18, 2023. This session was organised for SY and TY students. The objective of the session was to convey various platforms of TCS, like the coding hackathon, and give information about TCS infrastructure.
More than 100 students were present offline, and 700+ students attended the session online. Mr.Shashank Umbardand sir gave the brief of the TCS company, the services provided by the company, how the work is carried out at TCS, and many more. Mr.Hrishikesh Dhande sir gave information about the hackathons and coding carried out by the TCS; some of them are EngiNX, HackQuest, InfraMind, and HumAin. TCS also offers internships for 1st -year, 2nd-year, and 3rd-year students and placements for 4th -year students.The session concluded with a Q&A round.
