Session On Wolters Kluwer

The event organized by I2IC VIIT was conducted successfully on 7th December 2022 on behalf of Wolters Kluwer's on the topic "How are AI and data analytics transforming business problem solving". It was a great pleasure to have the speaker share his experience on the interesting topic. It special thanks to Mr. Abhishekh Mitthal to give his time and explain about AI and data analytics transforming.
When a data scientist interviews a line-of-business expert about a business problem, the data scientist listens fo key words or phrases. The data scientist breaks the problem into a process flow that always includes an understanding of the business problem, an understanding the data that is required, anf the types of artificial intelligence (AI) and Data Analytics techniques that can solve the problem. Together, this information drives an iterative set of thought experiments, modeling techniques, and evaluation against the business goals. The focus must stay on the business.
