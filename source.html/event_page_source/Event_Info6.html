AI and ML Hands On Workshop

The workshop for hands on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning for the VIIT students was organized by Institute to Industry Interaction Cell under the guidance of Training and Placement Cell VIIT. The speakers for the two days long workshop were Mr. Duraj Zala, Mr. Kunal Purohit, Mr. Sudip Das. All the speakers were from Smart Sense Solution Pvt. Ltd.
The session started by nice hosting of Payal and Gokul and Mr. Vishal Ambhore Sir inaugurated the session. Most important part of the session was second half of the day where the speakers conducted practical based approach on machine learning and deep learning. They explained pretrained model.

LTIMindTree Industry Visit

The first offline visit to LTIMindTree at Hinjewadi, Pune, under the supervision of Leena Deshpande Ma'am. Our faculties, Vikal Kolekar Sir, Pawan Wawge Sir, Radhika Puranadare Ma'am, Shilpa Patil Ma'am, Vijay Kumar Ghule Sir and Pravin Hujare Sir, were also present. The objective of the session was to make the students interact with experienced professionals under the Soft Skill Coliseum Programme.
Students also got to be aware of the work cycle of corporate offices, interact with working professionals, gain insights about work-life balance at LTIMindTree and much more. Students were taught the importance of effective communicatio, confidence and dressing. The training also discussed the common mistakes to avoid during the group discussion and the importance of following the rules and etiquette.
