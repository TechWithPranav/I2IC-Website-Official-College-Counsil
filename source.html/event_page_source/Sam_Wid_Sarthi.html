SAMWID SAARTHI ANGEL

"SAMWID - SAARTHI ANGEL 2023" hosted jointly by VIIT and MPF Uptown. The event featured a Job Flair and Internship Mela that are aimed at providing excellent opportunities for students to explore and enhace their career prospects. It is one of the major Flagship Event.
Day 1 :
On this special day, Bharat Agrawal Sir and Nidhi Ma'am shared their thoughts and motivated the students. After that, the placement and internship drive began. Interviews for every domain were conducted. The name of the companies that came on Day 1 are Technocore Logic LLP, ThinksmartIT Solution Private Ltd.,Lexicon, Novel Vista Learning Solutions, I Knowledge Factory Pvt. Ltd., Greenovative, Forya, Planedge, Shrikrishna Properties and Infrastructure, MySacredTrip, XClusive, Tesla Academy, BYJUS, GNR Solution, Pratha Naturals and Handmade Pvt. Ltd., Mascon Solution LLP, Nirmal Automation.
More than 850 students registered for interviews. 400+ interviews were successfully conducted on day one.

Day 2 :

The first guest speaker, Mr. Parag Shah, guided us through basic business plans. They conducted activities related to business plans that help build students visions in business. The second guest speaker was Mr. Anish Malpani, who invented the world's first recycled sunglasses from packets of chips. He told students about his journey. His speech inspired studnets to make sustainable products from waste.
The third guest speaker, Mr. Vikram Panit, talked about his journey. He is the regional director of the TUBACEX company. His valuable speech helps students at Startup. Lexicon did their pretalk session. They gave an overview of the company, then explained the job descriptions, and are also told about the selection process. 150+ students registered for interviews. More than 130 interviews were conducted on day two.
