The Training and Placement Department at VIIT facilitates career guidance, industry-relevant training, and job opportunities for students. Through collaboration with industry experts and alumni, the department ensures students gain the latest skills and knowledge. Regular activities such as aptitude training, tests, and GD&PI sessions prepare students for placements, internships, and competitive exams. The Training Placement Officer, along with department coordinators and student councils, oversees the operations. VIIT also organizes guest lectures and special training programs, leveraging its strong alumni network and partnerships with leading organizations for on-campus recruitment. As a result, VIIT is a preferred destination for companies seeking skilled professionals, reflecting its brand value and global alumni network.

Placement Stastics

Training Assesment

VIIT's Training & Placement Cell focuses on guiding students towards successful careers by starting training in the second year of their B.E. course. Through guest sessions and assessments like AMCAT, students are helped to identify suitable career paths based on their personalities. Over two years, workshops by professional institutes and industry experts cover various aspects of personality development, including coping with pressure, assertiveness, analytical and communication skills, and leadership development. VIIT encourages participation in quizzes, projects, and hackathons to enhance students' knowledge and overall personality. Additionally, to prepare students for higher education and global acceptance, training programs in areas like aptitude, logical reasoning, and English are conducted by trained faculty members. Emphasizing the importance of diverse training, guest faculties are invited to provide different perspectives, enriching the grooming process for future professionals.
