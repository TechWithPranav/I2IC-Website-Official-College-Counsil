about us

Welcome to the Industry to Institute Connection (I2IC) Club at VIIT, where innovation meets collaboration to bridge the gap between academia and industry.
At VIIT, we believe in cultivating a dynamic ecosystem where students can thrive by engaging with real-world challenges and industry leaders. The I2IC Club serves as a vibrant platform for students to explore, learn, and connect with the ever-evolving landscape of industries. Our mission is to foster a seamless transition from classroom learning to practical application, empowering our members to become future-ready professionals.
